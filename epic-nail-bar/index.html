Our Services

Crafted for every mood.

Premium Revival

Our signature spa experience. Choose Malibu Seabreeze (tropical coconut exfoliant) or Mint Mojito (CBD-infused cooling treatment). Includes callus treatment, 15-minute massage, neck wrap, paraffin, hot rocks, and warm towels. The full escape.

Pure Bliss

A tropical mini-getaway. Choose from Cucumber Cooler, Piña Colada, or Mangonada. Includes sugar scrub, paraffin, callus treatment, and an 8-minute massage. Perfect for a midweek reset.

Gel · Acrylic · Nail Art

From gel polish and Gel-X extensions to full acrylic sets and custom nail art. Our technicians bring your vision to life. Dip powder, French tips, and nail fixes available too.

Waxing & Facials

Complete your beauty routine without leaving the salon. We offer full-body waxing and facial treatments. Because self-care shouldn't require three stops — just one.
