What We Do

Asian Hair. Global Trends.

✦

Japanese Straightening

Thermal reconditioning for naturally straight, frizz-free hair that lasts. Ideal for thick, coarse, or wavy Asian hair. Our stylists have performed this treatment hundreds of times — results are smooth, natural, and long-lasting.

◎

Korean Digital Perm

Soft, defined waves or loose curls with that natural movement you see in K-drama. Digital perms use controlled heat to set the curl pattern exactly where you want it — bouncy at the ends, smooth at the roots. Great for adding texture and volume without looking stiff.

✂

K-Pop & Asian Haircuts

Layered cuts, curtain bangs, wolf cuts, hime cuts, and more — all styled to complement Asian hair texture and face shape. Our stylists stay current on trends from Seoul, Tokyo, and beyond. Bring your inspiration photos and we'll make it work for your actual hair type.

◈

Balayage & Color

Ash tones, vivid color, custom highlights, and natural-looking balayage — all done with deep understanding of how Asian hair lifts and takes color differently. Our colorists know how to get that soft, lived-in look without over-processing.

◇

Hair Restoration

Deep conditioning and damage repair for hair that's been over-bleached, heat-damaged, or broken down from previous chemical treatments. We assess the condition of your hair first and recommend a treatment plan that restores strength and shine.
