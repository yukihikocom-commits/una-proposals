What We Do

Full-service hair care
for the whole family

From a quick trim to a full color transformation — Van's has been taking care of Carrollton families since 2005.

Haircuts

Men's, women's, and kids' cuts — all done with care and attention to your individual style. Van takes the time to understand what you want and delivers a consistent result every single visit. Walk in or call ahead to put your name on the list.

Color & Highlights

Full color, highlights, balayage, and touch-up services that bring out the best in your hair. The team understands a wide range of hair types and textures, helping you achieve the exact tone and finish you have in mind — without damaging your hair in the process.

Perms & Styling

Traditional perms, body waves, and updo styling for special occasions — Van's brings technical skill and a personal touch to every service. Whether you're looking for soft curls, defined waves, or an elegant updo for an event, the results speak for themselves.

Shampoo & Hair Removal

Relaxing shampoo treatments to nourish and refresh your scalp, paired with professional hair removal services. A great add-on before any cut or color service, or as a standalone treatment when your hair needs some extra care between visits.

Our Story

“At Van's, we treat you like family — family that is glad to see you.”

Van's Hair Salon has been a fixture in the Carrollton community since 2005. What started as a small neighborhood salon has grown into one of the most trusted names in the area — not through advertising, but through word of mouth, one happy client at a time.

Van and the team bring real skill and genuine care to every chair. Whether you're in for a quick men's cut or a full color service, you'll leave feeling like you got exactly what you came for. That's been the promise since day one — and it hasn't changed.

Carrollton neighbors have made Van's a 12-time Nextdoor Neighborhood Favorite — an honor earned purely through consistent quality and a warm welcome for everyone who walks through the door.
