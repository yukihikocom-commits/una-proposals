What We Do

Every visit, a little more you.

Affordable doesn't mean ordinary. At Blossom Salon, precision and care come standard — from a $5 eyebrow thread to a full facial.

Threading

Precision brow shaping that lasts longer than waxing — and stays true to your natural arch. Our threading artists also handle upper lip, chin, full face, and sideburns. Saba and the team have perfected the technique over years of daily practice. Starting at $5 for eyebrows.

Waxing

Smooth, long-lasting results for every area — brows, legs, bikini, Brazilian, arms, back, and more. Shireen brings years of experience and a steady hand that clients come back to year after year. Sanitation protocols are strict and never skipped. Comfortable, private, and done right.

Facials

From the popular saffron facial to targeted skin treatments, every facial at Blossom Salon is customized to your skin type. The owner consults personally before each session. Clients describe the experience as pampering that actually works — glowing skin, visible results, and prices that make it a regular habit.
