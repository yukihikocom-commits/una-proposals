About

If hair could bring people together

That idea is where the name comes from — hair mates. Mitsuru cuts almost entirely by hand, guiding the scissors line by line rather than rushing, which is why the finish holds its shape as it grows out.

Years of work with every kind of hair — fine, thick, straight, wavy, and very dark — sit behind each appointment. Japanese straightening and digital perms are signature services, and color is done to lift tone while keeping hair healthy.

Because everything is by appointment, one guest has the chair at a time. You get the full attention, the consultation, and a result built around the photo you bring in.

Good to know

Frequently asked

What are your hours?

Monday, Thursday and Friday from 10 AM to 8 PM, and Saturday and Sunday from 10 AM to 7 PM. Closed Tuesday and Wednesday. Appointments are by request.

Where are you located, and is there parking?

Inside Phenix Salon Suites at 5964 W Parker Rd, Suite 116 / Room 114, Plano, TX 75093. There is free parking in the center's lot right outside the door.

Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?

Mitsuru works by appointment so each guest gets full, unhurried attention. Booking ahead is best, since the schedule fills up. If there's an opening, a walk-in can sometimes be taken.

What services do you offer?

Precision cuts for women, men and students; color including root touch-ups, single process and highlights; digital and men's perms; and Japanese straightening.

Do you specialize in Japanese straightening and perms?

Yes. Japanese straightening and digital perms are signature services here, done to leave hair smooth while protecting its condition.

Can I be helped in Japanese?

Yes. Japanese and English are both welcome, so you can describe exactly what you want in whichever language feels easier.

How much do services cost?

Cuts start around $50 for men and $60 for women. Color and perms depend on length and service, and Japanese straightening runs $260 to $350. Final pricing is confirmed at your consultation.

Do you have experience with Asian and textured hair?

Yes — extensive experience across hair types, from fine to thick and straight to wavy, including color work on very dark hair.

Is there another way to book besides calling?

Yes. Send a request through the form below or by email and you'll get a reply with open times — helpful when the phone is busy during an appointment.

What should I prepare for my first visit?

Bring a photo or two of the look you want. Mitsuru works from your reference to match the result closely, so clear pictures help a lot.
