Why Guests Keep Coming Back

The craft is
in every detail.

At Daily Bếp, nothing comes from a can or a shortcut. Every broth is simmered from bone. Every bánh mì is made to order. That's why guests drive from across DFW — and keep coming back.

Signature

Broth simmered from scratch

Our pho broth takes hours — every single day. No shortcuts, no concentrate. Just bones, spices, and time. You'll taste the difference the moment it hits the table. Guests describe it as the most authentic pho in DFW.

Fan Favorite

Bánh mì built to order

A proper bánh mì needs a proper baguette — crispy outside, pillowy inside. Ours are never made ahead. The Cheese Steak Bánh Mì is one of our most talked-about items, and regulars rarely order just one.

Drinks

Vietnamese beverages worth staying for

Our Vietnamese Iced Coffee is the real thing — bold, sweet, and smooth. Pair it with a Lychee Boba or a fresh Mango Lemonade. We take drinks as seriously as food, because a great meal deserves a great drink to finish.

Our Story

A family kitchen,
brought to Colleyville.

Daily Bếp started with a simple belief: Vietnamese cooking at its best is home cooking. The word "Bếp" means kitchen in Vietnamese — and that's exactly what we've built. A kitchen where everything matters.

You'll find spring-inspired decorations, warm lighting, and tables close enough to feel comfortable. It gets busy. But the service stays attentive because we know how much it means when someone trusts you with their dinner.
