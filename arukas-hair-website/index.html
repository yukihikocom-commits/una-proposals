What we do

Services shaped around your hair

Every head of hair is different. We start by listening, then choose the technique that suits your texture, your routine, and the look you have in mind.

Cut & Styling

A cut built around your face, your hair's movement, and how you actually style it at home. Our team is known for clean, confident lines — bangs and short styles handled with real care. You leave with a shape that still looks right days later.

Color & Balayage

Highlights, balayage, ombre, and full color — plus vivid, fashion-forward shades when you want to make a statement. We use Olaplex through bleach and lightening work, so your hair stays as healthy as it looks. Bring a photo and we'll build the tone with you.

Japanese Straight Perm

One of our signatures. A gentle, carefully controlled Japanese straight perm that smooths and relaxes texture for long-lasting, manageable hair. We tailor the strength to your hair's condition so the result is soft and natural — never flat or over-processed.

Our story

A Japanese salon, in the heart of Plano

ARUKAS opened in Plano in 2017, part of the Arukas Hair family that began in California. Our stylists are all Japanese, all English-speaking, and all trained in the discipline and detail that Japanese salon work is known for.

We're a small, personable studio — the kind of place where you're remembered, where a warm towel and a gentle head massage are simply part of the visit. Whether you want something kawaii and playful or clean and refined, we take the time to get it right.

Good to know

Questions guests ask us

What are your hours?
We're open Tuesday through Saturday, 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM, and Sunday 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. We're closed on Mondays.
Where are you located, and is there parking?
We're at 100 Legacy Dr, Suite 101, Plano, TX 75023, in the Legacy Central plaza near Mitsuwa Marketplace. Parking is available at the plaza lot.
Do I need an appointment, or can I walk in?
We work by appointment so we can give each guest our full attention. You can book online anytime, and if you'd like to come in the same day, please call us and we'll do our best to fit you in.
What services do you offer?
Cut and styling, color, highlights, balayage, ombre, bleach with Olaplex care, and Japanese straight perm. We work with all hair types and love creating both kawaii and cool, clean looks.
What are you known for?
Japanese straight perm and color work are two of our signatures. Our team also has a light, careful touch with bleach and treatments to keep your hair healthy.
Can I be served in Japanese?
Yes. Our stylists are all Japanese and speak both Japanese and English, so you can relax and describe exactly what you want in whichever language feels natural.
How much do services cost?
Pricing depends on the service, your hair length, and the look you're going for. We'll always talk it through and give you a clear estimate before we start, so there are no surprises.
Do you have experience with Asian hair?
Absolutely. Our whole team is trained in Japanese salon technique and works with Asian hair every day, so we understand its texture, density, and how it responds to color and perms.
What should I bring to my first visit?
Reference photos are the best thing to bring — a few pictures of styles or colors you like help us understand your goal quickly. Beyond that, just come as you are and we'll take care of the rest.
What makes ARUKAS different?
We've been caring for Plano hair since 2017 as an all-Japanese team. You get precise Japanese craft in a small, personable salon where the details — like a warm towel and a gentle head massage — are part of the experience.
