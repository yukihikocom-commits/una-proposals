Area guide

Dallas–Fort Worth highlights

The Dallas–Fort Worth area is remarkably livable — a big metro, yet relatively safe and affordable, with no state income tax. Popular suburbs sit within easy reach of major employers and are welcoming to newcomers from out of state.

Featured areas

Plano

Beautifully balanced between a calm feel and convenience, and a longtime favorite among families. Top-rated schools, abundant shopping and dining, and easy access to major employers.

Frisco

A fast-growing city especially popular with families. Newly built throughout, with clean, safe neighborhoods, top-rated schools, and shopping, sports, and entertainment close at hand.

Prosper

An area of rising interest, popular with out-of-state movers. Its school district ranks among the best in Texas, with appealing master-planned communities like Windsong Ranch and Mustang Lakes.

Areas served — Plano · Frisco · Prosper

Centered on Frisco, from Plano in the south to Prosper in the north, with service across the wider DFW metroplex.
